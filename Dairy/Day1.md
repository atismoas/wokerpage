

As Einstenid said:" Not everything that counts can be counted, and not everything that's counted truly counts."

“并非所有重要的东西都是可以被计算的，也不是所有能被计算的东西都那么重要。”


Wood splintered and vines tore until the enormous stalk cracked in two;

木头碎裂，藤曼撕裂，巨大的茎秆断成两截；


Everything else fades into background when your body breaks down.

当身体崩溃时，其他的一切都退居幕后。


No one is too old to grow, to learn, to begin again;

并不是太老就不能成长，学习和重新开始。


The life you want is not found in the crowd, it's waiting where few are willing to go.

你想要的生活不在人群之中，它在很少人愿意去的地方等待；
